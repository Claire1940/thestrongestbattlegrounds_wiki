  <Step num="3" title="Genos' Ignition Burst Combo">

  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Atomic Samurai's Quick Slices">
    Initiate with a dash, then M1s, followed by **Pinpoint Cuts**. Dash again, use **Atoms Cleave**, a quick M1 down slam, and **Quick Slice**. If you land the final dash, you can extend the combo for a kill.
  </Step>
