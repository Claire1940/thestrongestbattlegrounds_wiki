To maximize your damage with **the strongest battlegrounds wild psychic**, you must utilize your combo extenders effectively. Here is a standard high-damage rotation for 2026.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The M1 Opener">
    Land 3 M1 hits. Since your M1s are the fastest, you can usually win the neutral game. Do not finish the 4th hit yet.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Uppercut Dash">

  <Step num="3" title="Stone Coffin Extension">

  <Step num="4" title="Crushing Pull Reset">

  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Windstorm Fury Finisher">

  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Combo Pro-Tip">
You can perform up to three Uppercut Dashes in one sequence: one at the start, one post-Stone Coffin, and one timed with Windstorm Fury. This makes Wild Psychic's combos some of the longest in the game.
