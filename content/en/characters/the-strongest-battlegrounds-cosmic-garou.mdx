  <Step num="1" title="Activate Monster Garou Ultimate">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Apply Hunter's Mark">

This sequence is visually spectacular, showcasing Garou's evolution into his cosmic state.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Embrace Cosmic Garou">

  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Precise Timing Required">
