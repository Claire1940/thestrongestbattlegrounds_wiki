<Section icon={Target} title="How to Execute a Perfect Wall Assault">

Executing **the strongest battlegrounds wall combo** requires precise timing and spatial awareness. You cannot simply mash buttons; you must wait for the specific "stun" window after your final basic strike. The most common mistake players make is dashing too early or too late, which results in a standard forward dash attack rather than the cinematic wall combo.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Pin the Opponent">
    Use your M1 attacks or a mobility move to push your enemy toward a solid surface. They must be within a few studs of the wall for the game to register the collision.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Land the Trigger Strike">
    Perform either a full 4-hit M1 chain, an uppercut, or a downslam. These specific moves prime the opponent for the wall interaction.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Input the Forward Dash">
    Approximately **0.2 seconds** after the final hit, perform a Forward Dash (W + Q on PC, or the Dash button on Mobile). If timed correctly, the screen will shift to the wall combo animation.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Prepare the Follow-up">
    Most wall combos knock the opponent down or bounce them back. Use the 6-second cooldown period to position yourself for your next skill activation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Metric | Value | Details |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Wall Combo Damage** | **12%** | Fixed damage across all standard characters |
| **Cooldown Duration** | **5-6 Seconds** | Prevents infinite wall-looping |
| **Timing Window** | **~0.2 Seconds** | Must be input immediately after the M1 finisher |
| **Execution Range** | **Close Proximity** | Opponent must be touching the wall |

<Callout type="warning" title="Critical Timing Alert">
If you miss the 4th M1 punch or it is blocked, you will be stunned for **1.0 second**. During this time, you are vulnerable to a counter-attack and cannot initiate the wall combo.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Armor, Hyperarmor, and Defensive Properties">

A major reason to master the wall combo is the defensive utility it provides. During the animation of most wall combos, your character gains **Armor**. This means you cannot be interrupted by basic M1 attacks from other players who might be trying to "peel" for their teammate.

| Protection Type | Effect | Wall Combo Relevance |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Armor** | Immune to M1 stun/damage | Granted to most characters during the animation |
| **Hyperarmor** | Immune to all damage/skills | Granted to **KJ** during his wall combo |
| **I-Frames** | Completely invulnerable | Rare, usually reserved for Ultimate startups |
| **Armor Break** | Bypasses Armor | Some Ultimate moves can interrupt a wall combo |

Understanding these properties allows you to use a wall combo as a "safe" period in a chaotic 2v2 or free-for-all scenario. However, be aware of "Armor Break" moves which can still damage you through your animation.
