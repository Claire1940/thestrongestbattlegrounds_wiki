<Section icon={Target} title="Speed and Precision: Ninja and Blade Master">

Deadly Ninja (Sonic) and Blade Master (Atomic Samurai) are the preferred choices for players who value speed over bulk. Their combos are often "unescapable" if timed correctly, as they rely on stun frames that prevent the opponent from activating their block.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initiate with Dash">
    Always start with a Front Dash or Side Dash to bypass the initial block. For Blade Master, a Quick Slice is an excellent opener.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The M1 Buffer">
    Perform exactly 3 M1 hits. The 4th hit in a standard chain usually knocks the opponent away, ending your combo prematurely.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Ability Weaving">
    For Sonic, use *Scatter* or *Explosive Shuriken* (Air variant) immediately after the 3rd M1. For Blade Master, use *Atmos Cleave* to keep the target airborne.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Catch">
    As the opponent falls or staggers, use a Side Dash to "catch" them before they hit the ground. This allows you to start a fresh 3M1 chain.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Blade Master Precision">
The "Flame Nova" combo (Atmos Cleave into Downslam) requires perfect timing. You must start the Downslam wind-up while still mid-air to catch the opponent exactly as they begin their recovery animation.
</Callout>
