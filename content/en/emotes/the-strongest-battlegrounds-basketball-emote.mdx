<Section icon={Target} title="How to Acquire the Basketball Emote">
Acquiring new emotes in *The Strongest Battlegrounds* typically involves either earning enough kills in-game or purchasing them with Robux. The RAHHH basketball emote falls under this general acquisition system.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Earn Kills">
    To unlock emotes through gameplay, you generally need to accumulate 50 kills. Each player kill grants 1 kill point, while using a finisher on an opponent grants 2 kill points. Some large Area-of-Effect (AOE) skills can also provide 2 extra kills.
    <Callout type="warning">
    Kills earned in private servers do **NOT** count towards emote progression. However, friendly duels can still contribute to your kill count.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Utilize Destructive Cyborg (Optional)">
    For efficient kill farming, consider using the **Destructive Cyborg** character. This character's passive ability resets cooldowns upon performing a finisher, significantly speeding up the process of accumulating kills.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Claim the Emote">
    Once you have accumulated the required 50 kills, you can claim a random emote. Emotes are obtained randomly, not through a specific probability for each one. If you already own all available emotes, the "Claim emote here" button will disappear.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Purchase with Robux">
    Alternatively, emotes can be purchased directly using Robux. The typical cost for a single emote is 25 Robux. There are also bundles available for 125 Robux (5 emotes), 250 Robux (10 emotes), and 1250 Robux (50 emotes).
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
Emotes are drawn randomly when claimed via kills or purchased. You cannot choose a specific emote like RAHHH directly unless it is part of a special offer.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="General Emote Mechanics and Customization">
Emotes in *The Strongest Battlegrounds* are a significant part of player expression and interaction. Understanding their mechanics can enhance your gameplay experience.

**Emote Wheel and Slots:**
The emote wheel is accessed by pressing the 'B' key.
Players start with 4 emote slots.
You can expand your emote capacity:
- **Extra Emote Slots Gamepass**: Adds 4 more slots, totaling 8.
- **Emote Second Page Gamepass**: Provides an entire second page for even more emotes, potentially allowing up to 16 slots if both gamepasses are purchased.

| Emote Feature | Description | Cost/Requirement |
| :------------ | :---------- | :--------------- |
| Emote Wheel   | Access point for all emotes | 'B' key          |
| Default Slots | Number of initial emote slots | 4                |
| Extra Slots   | Gamepass to expand emote capacity | 99 Robux         |
| Second Page   | Gamepass for an additional page of emotes | 99 Robux         |

**Canceling Emotes:**
Emotes are not permanent actions and can be interrupted by various player actions or external factors:
- Getting hit by another player
- Jumping
- Blocking
- Dashing
- Punching
- Using another emote

<Callout type="warning">
Be mindful of when you use emotes, especially in combat situations, as they can leave you vulnerable if not canceled effectively.
</Callout>
