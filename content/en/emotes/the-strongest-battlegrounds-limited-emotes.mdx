<Section icon={Target} title="Acquiring Limited Emotes: A Step-by-Step Guide">
Obtaining a **limited emote** in The Strongest Battlegrounds requires a keen eye on the in-game shop and a supply of Robux. These emotes are not always available, emphasizing the importance of checking the shop regularly.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Monitor the Emote Shop">
    Regularly check the in-game emote shop, usually accessible via the **'B' key**, for new rotations. Limited emotes are clearly marked with a "LIMITED" message and often an associated Robux cost. Shop rotations typically occur weekly.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Preview the Emote">
    Before purchasing, click on the gray eye icon next to the emote's listing in the emote wheel. This will often show a video preview of how the emote plays out, allowing you to decide if it's worth the Robux.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Purchase with Robux">
    If you decide to acquire a **limited emote**, ensure you have sufficient Robux. Click the purchase button, and the emote will be added to your collection. Remember, these are premium items and cannot be obtained through normal gameplay or kills.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Equip and Showcase">
    Once purchased, you can equip your new limited emote to one of your available emote slots. You start with 4 slots and can purchase an "Extra Emote Slots Gamepass" for 4 more, totaling 8. Now you're ready to showcase your unique emote in battles or social hubs!
  </Step>
</Steps>
