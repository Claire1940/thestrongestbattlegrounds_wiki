<Section icon={Shield} title="Advanced Device & In-Game Optimizations">
Beyond basic graphics, there are several advanced settings on your mobile device and within The Strongest Battlegrounds that can further combat lag. These adjustments target background processes and specific game elements that might be consuming valuable processing power.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Disable Location Services">
    Navigate to your device's privacy settings and turn off Location Services. This feature constantly uses energy and processing power, and disabling it can lead to an instant improvement in FPS.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Enable Fast Mode in-game">
    Inside The Strongest Battlegrounds, look for the "Fast Mode" option. Ensure this setting is turned on (green). Fast Mode significantly reduces particle effects, craters, smoke, and other visual clutter, which are major contributors to lag during intense combat.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Reduce Camera Shake">
    Also within the game settings, enable "Reduce Camera Shake." As its name suggests, this minimizes camera movements and shakes during chaotic moments, providing a more stable visual experience and potentially reducing processing strain.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Optimize DNS Settings (Advanced)">
    For persistent network-related lag, consider changing your device's DNS. Go to your Wi-Fi settings, select the connected network, and change DNS to manual. Delete existing servers and input a new, potentially faster DNS server address. This can improve connection stability and reduce latency.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="DNS Change Caution">
Modifying DNS settings is an advanced step. If unsure, consult reliable guides or your internet service provider. Incorrect DNS settings can lead to connectivity issues.
</Callout>
