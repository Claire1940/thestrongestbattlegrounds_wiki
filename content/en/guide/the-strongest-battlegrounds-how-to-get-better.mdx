### Essential Combat Techniques
Effective combat in **the strongest battlegrounds** is a blend of offense and defense. Knowing when to strike and when to evade is critical.

| Technique       | Description                                       | Primary Use                                     | Risk Level |
| :-------------- | :------------------------------------------------ | :---------------------------------------------- | :--------- |
| **Simple Combos** | Short, reliable attack sequences.                 | Consistent damage, low chance of being punished. | Low        |
| **Side Dashing**  | Quick sidestep towards or away from opponent.    | Initiate combos, dodge attacks, create openings. | Medium     |
| **Counter Dashing** | Dashing into opponent's dash direction.         | Get behind opponent for a counter-attack.       | Medium     |
| **Ragdoll Cancel**| Canceling ragdoll state to recover.              | Avoid extended combos, regain control.          | High       |
| **Blocking**      | Holding block to negate incoming damage.         | Defensive stance, predict opponent's moves.     | Low        |

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid Fancy Combos">
While flashy, complex combos may seem impressive, they are often high-risk and easily interrupted in **the strongest battlegrounds**. Focus on short, efficient combos that guarantee damage and are less prone to errors under pressure. Consistency trumps flair.
