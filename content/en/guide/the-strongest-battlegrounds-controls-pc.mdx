### Basic Attack Types and Inputs

The foundation of combat in The Strongest Battlegrounds relies on your basic attacks, often referred to as M1s (Mouse Button 1). These can be chained into various combos for different effects.

| Attack Type | PC Input | Effect | Key Use |

<Section icon={Shield} title="Dashing and Defensive Maneuvers">
Dashing is an indispensable tool for both offense and defense in The Strongest Battlegrounds. It allows you to quickly close distances, evade incoming attacks, and reposition yourself strategically. Blocking, on the other hand, is your primary defensive action to mitigate damage.

<Callout type="warning" title="Forward Dash Vulnerability">
While the **Forward Dash** (W + Q) is great for closing distance, it leaves a brief window of vulnerability (roughly half a second) where opponents can freely attack you before you can retaliate. Use it cautiously or as a surprise opener.
</Callout>

### Dash Types and Usage

Each dash type serves a specific purpose and has different properties. Understanding when to use each can significantly impact your survivability and combo potential.

| Dash Type | PC Input | Cooldown | Primary Use |
|---|---|---|---|
| Forward Dash | W + Q | 5 seconds | Initiating combat, closing distance |
| Side Dash | A/D + Q | 2 seconds | Evasion, repositioning, combo extension |
| Back Dash | S + Q | 5 seconds | Escaping wide attacks, creating space |

Side dashing is often considered the most versatile dash due to its lower cooldown and lack of vulnerability after use. It's excellent for weaving in and out of combat and extending complex combos.

### Blocking Mechanics

Blocking is a simple yet effective defensive control.

| Action | PC Input | Effect | Limitations |
|---|---|---|---|
| Block | Hold F | Protects from basic attacks & some moves (180°) | Significantly slower movement, cannot perform other actions, some attacks break block |

<Callout type="success" title="Critical Blocking">
Perfectly timing your block against an opponent's basic attack (M1) can result in a "critical hit" on your next basic attack, dealing triple damage. Master this to turn defense into offense!
</Callout>
