<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Step-by-Step Activation of Shift Lock">
Enabling shift lock is a straightforward process that can be done quickly within the game. Follow these steps to ensure you have this crucial feature activated for your next battle.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Access the Roblox Menu">
    While in The Strongest Battlegrounds, locate the Roblox menu icon in the top-left corner of your screen and click it. Alternatively, you can simply press the **Escape (Esc)** key on your keyboard. This action will open the game's main menu.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Navigate to Settings">
    Within the opened menu, you will see several tabs. Click on the **"Settings"** tab, usually found at the top. This section contains various configurable options for your gameplay experience.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Enable Shift Lock Switch">
    On the settings page, the very first option listed should be the **"Shift Lock Switch"**. Toggle this setting to **"On"**. This action activates the shift lock functionality.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Resume Game">
    After enabling the switch, click the **"Resume"** button at the bottom of the menu to return to your game. You can now press the **Shift** key on your keyboard to activate and deactivate shift lock during gameplay.
  </Step>
</Steps>
