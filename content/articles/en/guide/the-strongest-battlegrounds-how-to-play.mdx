<Section icon={Shield} title="Defensive and Evasive Maneuvers">
Effective defense and evasion are just as important as offense in The Strongest Battlegrounds. Knowing when to block, dash, or counter can turn the tide of any fight. These techniques allow you to mitigate incoming damage, reposition yourself, and create openings for your own attacks.

<Callout type="warning" title="Blocking Limitations">
While blocking is powerful, it significantly slows your movement and prevents other actions. Some powerful attacks, like Normal Punch (up close), Shove, Uppercut, Flowing Water, and Hunter's Grasp, can break through your block.
</Callout>

### Dashing Mechanics

Dashes are versatile tools for movement, evasion, and initiating combos. Each type of dash serves a distinct purpose.

| Dash Type | Input (PC) | Cooldown | Range | Primary Use |
| :-------- | :--------- | :------- | :---- | :---------- |
| **Forward Dash** | W + Q | 5 seconds | 4.5 tiles | Quickly close distance, initiate combos (can leave you vulnerable) |
| **Side Dash** | A/D + Q | 2 seconds | 3 tiles | Evade attacks, reposition, most versatile for advanced combos |
| **Back Dash** | S + Q | 5 seconds | 4.5 tiles | Escape wide-covering attacks (like Ultimates), create distance |

<Callout type="info">
Side Dashes are generally considered the best due to their lower cooldown and lack of a vulnerable window after use. They are client-sided, ensuring zero delay on your screen regardless of ping.
</Callout>

### Blocking and Counter-Attacks

Holding the "F" key (PC) or the block button (mobile) allows you to block. This protects you from basic attacks and some moves from a 180-degree angle. However, you move slower and cannot perform other actions.

Critical Hits and Black Flashes are advanced techniques that significantly boost your damage output.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Achieve a Critical Hit">
    Perfectly block an opponent's M1 attack. You'll hear a subtle sound cue. Immediately follow up with your own M1 attack. This will deal triple damage (10% instead of 3%).
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Unleash a Black Flash">
    After successfully landing a Critical Hit, perform another perfect block (without being ragdolled). Your next M1 attack will trigger a "Black Flash," indicated by black and red particles, dealing six times the normal M1 damage (up to 18%).
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Strategic Transfer">
    A Black Flash can be transferred to a different target if you hit them instead of your original opponent, offering flexibility in team fights or when switching targets.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Wall Combos

Wall Combos are specialized attacks that utilize the environment to deal extra damage. Each character has a unique wall combo.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Position Your Opponent">
    Maneuver your enemy against a wall, tree, or similar object.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Execute a Setup Move">
    Land the 4th M1 attack, a Downslam, or an Uppercut.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Follow with a Dash">
    Immediately after the setup move (approximately 0.2 seconds), perform a Forward Dash. This will trigger your character's unique Wall Combo, dealing an additional 12% damage.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip">
Wall Combos have a 5-second cooldown. Use them strategically when you have an opponent cornered to maximize damage.
</Callout>

### Combat Status Effects

| Effect Type | Description | Trigger Examples | Strategic Use |
| :---------- | :---------- | :--------------- | :------------ |
| **Stun** | Prevents movement, attacks, or blocking for a short duration. | Consecutive Punches, missed 4th M1, certain character abilities. | Create openings for follow-up attacks or to escape. |
| **Armor** | Grants immunity to regular M1 and most skills, prevents stun. | Wall Combos, Downslam (finisher), specific character moves (e.g., Hero Hunter's Flowing Water). | Absorb damage during critical moments, power through enemy attacks. |
| **Hyperarmor (I-Frames)** | Complete invincibility and interruption immunity for a short period. | Most Ultimate startups (except The Strongest Hero), KJ's Wall Combo, specific character finishers. | Dodge powerful attacks, ensure your critical moves land without interruption. |
| **Ragdoll** | Knocks opponent down, preventing any action for ~2 seconds. | End of most M1 strings, many finishing moves. | Control fight pace, set up further combos, or create distance. |
| **Roll** | Forces character into a rolling animation, covering distance. | Specific hits that cause rolling. | Evade follow-up attacks, can be prolonged if hit while airborne. |

</Section>
