### Private Server Setup for Observation

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Acquire Private Server Gamepass">
    Purchase the "Private Server+" gamepass from The Strongest Battlegrounds' Roblox page. This is a one-time purchase that grants you the ability to create and manage private servers.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Create a Private Server">
    From the game's main menu, select the option to create a private server. You will be the host and have administrative control over the server settings.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Invite Players">
    Invite the players you wish to observe into your private server. They can join using a unique server code or through your Roblox friends list.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Set Up Match Conditions">
    Once players are in the server, agree on the match conditions (e.g., 1v1, 2v2, specific characters). You can then observe their battles without participating directly.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Observe and Analyze">
    Move around the map freely to get different perspectives of the fights. Pay attention to movement, ability usage, and combo chains.
  </Step>
</Steps>
