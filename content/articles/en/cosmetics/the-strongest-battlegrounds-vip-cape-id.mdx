### Uploading Your Own Custom Capes

If you can't find a decal that suits your taste, you have the option to create and upload your own custom image.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Access Roblox Creator Hub">
    Go to the Roblox Creator Hub website. This is where you manage your creations and assets.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Upload Your Image">
    Navigate to "Development Items" and select the "Decals" option. Here, you can upload your custom image file. Ensure it meets Roblox's content guidelines.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Retrieve Asset ID">
    After uploading, go to the "Images" section under "Development Items." Find your newly uploaded image. Click on the three-striped dots (ellipsis) next to it and copy the asset ID. This is your unique **VIP Cape ID**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Apply in Game">
    Return to The Strongest Battlegrounds and follow the steps outlined previously to paste your new custom cape ID into the settings menu.
  </Step>
</Steps>
