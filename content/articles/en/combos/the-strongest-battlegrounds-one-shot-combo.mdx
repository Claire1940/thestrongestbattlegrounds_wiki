<Section icon={Target} title="The Strongest Hero (Saitama) One-Shot Combo">
Saitama, known as The Strongest Hero, possesses several powerful moves that can be chained into devastating combos. While his ultimate moves are often one-shot kills on their own, his regular state combos can also be lethal if executed precisely. The key is to keep the opponent locked down and unable to use their Evasive Dash.

Here's a highly effective combo for Saitama:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initiate with Down Slam">
    Start with a **Down Slam** to launch the opponent.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Follow with Uppercut and Punches">
    Immediately follow with an **Uppercut**, then land **Consecutive Punches**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Mini Uppercut & Shove">
    Execute a **Mini Uppercut**, followed by a **Shove**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Dash and Finish">
    Dash towards the opponent, perform **M1, M1, M1**, then another **Mini Uppercut**, and finally a **Normal Punch** to finish the sequence.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Evasive Dash Counter">
This combo relies on the opponent not having their Evasive Dash available. Always be mindful of your opponent's cooldowns and try to bait out their escape options before committing to a full combo.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Hero Hunter (Garou) One-Shot Combo Strategies">
Garou, the Hero Hunter, is a challenging character to master but rewards skilled players with potent combos. The "Twisted Tech" is crucial for extending combos with Garou.

**Garou's Regular State Combo (High Damage):**

| Sequence                                | Notes                                                               |
|-----------------------------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **3x M1** + **Twisted Tech**            | Crucial for extending the combo, requires practice.                 |
| **Flowing Water**                       | Launch into the air.                                                |
| **Down Slam**                           | Bring them back down.                                               |
| **Hunter's Grasp** (cancel throw)       | Grab and immediately cancel the throw to keep them close.           |
| **Punch** (immediately after cancel)    | Follow up with a quick punch.                                       |

<Callout type="info" title="Twisted Tech Explained">
The Twisted Tech involves performing three M1s, backing up on the fourth, and then dashing back in. This technique helps reset hitstun and extends combo potential. It takes time to learn but significantly improves Garou's combo game.
</Callout>
