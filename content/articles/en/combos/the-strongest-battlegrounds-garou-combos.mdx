  <Step num="1" title="The Dash Catch">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Mini-Uppercut Transition">
    Immediately after the 3rd M1, perform a **Mini-Uppercut**. This keeps the opponent in the air and prevents them from instantly ragdolling.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Skill Integration">
    While they are mid-air, use **Flowing Water** or **Lethal Whirlwind Stream**. If you are near a wall, use **Hunter's Grasp** to slam them against it for a wall combo.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Downslam Finisher">
    End the sequence with a **Downslam** while they are ragdolled. If you have **Prey's Peril** ready, wait for them to stand up and use it if they attempt a counter-attack.
  </Step>
</Steps>
