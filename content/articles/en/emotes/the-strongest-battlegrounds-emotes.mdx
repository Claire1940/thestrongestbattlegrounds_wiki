In high-level play, **the strongest battlegrounds emotes** are not just for show; they are vital tools for competitive optimization.

By activating an emote (like "Heh" or "Footwork") at the exact moment of awakening, players can skip the lengthy animation, allowing them to attack immediately. This often catches opponents off-guard.

**Visual Deception**
