<Section icon={Sparkles} title="KJ's Origin and Trivia">
KJ's presence in The Strongest Battlegrounds is rich with unique lore and interesting facts, distinguishing it from other characters in the game. From its animated inspirations to its musical themes, KJ is a character with a story.

**Origin and Inspiration:**
KJ is based on the popular "KJ series" of animations, primarily animated by Stealth, and "KJ's Final Ride" animated by King from Hyun's Dojo. The character's voice actor is Sora The Troll, a content creator, voice actor, and translator.

| Aspect | Detail |
| :----- | :----- |
| **Animated Basis** | KJ series (Stealth), KJ's Final Ride (King from Hyun's Dojo) |
| **Voice Actor** | Sora The Troll (西松清己) |
| **Creator** | DaSassyOwl (main animator of the KJ series), not KiloJoule |
| **Name Speculation** | Community speculated "KiloJoule," "Kinder Joy," "Killer Joy," or "Kenji," but all were debunked by DaSassyOwl. |

<Callout type="info" title="Did You Know?">
KJ is the first and only character in The Strongest Battlegrounds to feature voice lines in its moves and awakening, adding another layer of uniqueness to its gameplay experience.
</Callout>

**Unique In-Game Features:**
- **Unannounced Character**: KJ is unique as it was never officially announced before its release.
- **Numbered Move**: "20-20-20 Dropkick" is the first and only move in the game to contain numbers in its name.
- **Spawn Animation**: KJ's distinctive spawn animation involves a "Phone 2.0" breaking in half as Day leaps out.
- **Musical Themes**:
  - **20 Series Theme**: "E-ZUKA - Vital Mission In The Spanish Desert" (from the Needless OST), used in "KJ's Final Ride."
  - **Unlimited Flex Works Theme**: "Dive!! - A-One Studio," an OST made specifically for The Strongest Battlegrounds.

**Past Availability:**
While primarily exclusive, there was a period from December 21st, 2024, to May 24th, 2025, where KJ was temporarily free in "KJ Servers" following The Strongest Battlegrounds winning a Roblox Innovation Award. As of May 24, 2026, KJ Servers have been removed, making the character only accessible via private servers or for developers/top donators.

### Countering KJ and Common Pitfalls
While powerful, KJ is not invincible. Understanding its weaknesses can help both KJ players and their opponents.

- **Base Moveset Vulnerability**: The start-up animations for **Ravage** and **Swift Sweep** can be cancelled, creating opportunities for opponents.
- **Combo Extension Difficulty**: Due to heavy knockback on many moves, chaining combos can be challenging. **Swift Sweep** is the primary combo extender.
- **Ultimate Counterplay**: Be aware that **Five Seasons** and **Stoic Bomb** can be countered by specific abilities.
- **Stoic Bomb Positioning**: The damage of **Stoic Bomb** scales with proximity. It's most effective when used after an opponent is popped up (e.g., by an uppercut or downslam) to ensure maximum impact.
