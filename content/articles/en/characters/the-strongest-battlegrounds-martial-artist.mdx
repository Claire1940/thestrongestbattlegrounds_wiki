### The Uptilting Beast Passive

The **Uptilting Beast** passive significantly enhances your mini-uppercut. After a Finisher, your next mini-uppercut becomes a "dragonic uppercut," dealing additional damage (up to 16.5%) and bypassing block. During your ultimate, this effect has unlimited uses. This passive is crucial for extending combos and dealing burst damage. The best way to leverage this is to hit with **Vanishing Kick**, M1 into the mini-uppercut, and then use **Vanishing Kick** again as they fly away.

<Callout type="info" title="Passive Synergy">
The **Uptilting Beast** passive synergizes perfectly with **Vanishing Kick** for powerful combo extensions, even without your ultimate. Practice this sequence to maximize your damage output.
</Callout>
